Beta Testers Needed! Email #52

Note from Raelyn: A great way to kick-start any product/service is to take in a round of beta students to get some testimonials first. These beta testers will go through your program, give you valuable feedback, AND give you a raving testimonial that you can use in the future. This email helps you to get beta testers. Note: You probably want to send this to existing customers or a small segment of your list only - and do this only for products that are digital and/or are at a lower price point.

Subject Line: Beta Testers Needed! Email Copy:
Hey [first name],
I hope you've been well!

I thought about extending this to you since you're a valued [subscriber / customer]. :)
Not sure if you want to beta test my upcoming [describe what you want to give them access to] for free?
I'm actually just ready to launch [product name] right now and need beta reviewers. [Product name] consists of:

● [Describe all the amazing things they will get if they agree to be a beta tester]

●  [Describe all the amazing things they will get if they agree to be a beta tester]


●  [Describe all the amazing things they will get if they agree to be a beta tester]


Would you like to get early access to the full [product name] (just need a video testimonial)! I will be selling it for somewhere between $[#] - $[#].


Let me know soon because I only have a couple of slots for this and need people to quickly access it, use it, and say something nice about it (if you like it) within a week from now! :)


Don't worry, it's just a short one that won't even take 5 minutes of your time (recommended video length is 1 - 3 minutes). You can record from your computer or phone very easily. I'll provide guiding questions too.


Just hit reply to this email and let me know if you're interested!
