Call for questions!

I feel much more motivation & clarity when I have a specific audience in mind, or a specific question to answer. 

I want to make a call for questions about English conversation in the VFN:

Call for Questions:
  • Hello everyone I am in the process of creating an online community for English conversation. I invite you to check it out!
  • What are your questions about English, specifically English conversation?
  • What are your struggles when learning to speak English?
  • Why have you stopped in the past learning to speak English?
  • What do you want to achieve by learning to speak English?
  • What do you like with the offerings out there when you learn to speak English?
  • What do you dislike with the offerings out there when you learn to speak English?
