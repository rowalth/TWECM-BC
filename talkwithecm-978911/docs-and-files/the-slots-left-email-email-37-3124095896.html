The [#] Slots Left Email: Email #37

Note from Raelyn: People are very used to seeing a “X hours left” email, but you rarely see a “X slots left” email. Use this email to emphasize that you only have a limited number of slots left and get people to act now. Why does this work? 1) People like the exclusivity. 2) By saying that you are only working with X number of people (even if this isn’t a 1:1 program), this focuses on that fact that you are running a high-touch program. This assures people that you will show up for them and they won’t be lost in a crowd of students. 3) In-built urgency that doesn’t sound sleazy or fake... because you really DO only have X number of slots left.

This can be used for 1:1 coaching, group coaching, or courses that have some kind of live support to it. Send this towards the end of your launch.

Subject Line: [#] slots left!

Email Copy:

Hey [first name],

I’m looking to help [#] [describe your audience - e.g. entrepreneurs / doctors / lawyers / etc.] to achieve [end result].

Here are some details:

●  [Describe your offer. Why is it so amazing? How will you be helping your customers achieve their end goal?]
