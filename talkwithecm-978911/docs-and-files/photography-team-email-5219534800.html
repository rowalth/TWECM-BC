Welcome to the 
VFN Professionals
Photography Team! 


Hello there,

Thanks for volunteering to be part of our team to help us document our events and shape our public image. 

I'd like us to meet as a team once to sit down & discuss how we can best do this. I want to hear your voices as well as include your ideas and experience. 

As a starting point, I want to talk about the following 3 areas of responsibility:

1. Creating guidelines
- develop & follow clear guidelines about what we want 
- decide how to deal with social media / posting / privacy
- mindful of consent (especially for children!)

2. Document events
- create schedule/ sign up sheet?
- make sure at least one person is taking photos at every event
- decide how to deliver images to (me) shared space

3. Create more representative image(s) for VFN Pros - for email header/fb/ etc.

So, let's have a short meeting to get to know each other and plan before our next VFN Professionals meeting - October 24.

I suggest meeting at Al Chile (http://www.al-chile.info).

Please use this link - http://whenisgood.net/Photo-team
and mark the times that you are available.

Looking forward,
ecm

3. This link is for your personal use only. Do not share this link with anyone. Please download the photos relating to you and your family, these you may share.

4. If you choose to share VFN Party photos of your own family on social media, please give photo credit  to the photographer when sharing (name is on folder) and to tag us @Viennafamilynetwork ( www.facebook.com/viennafamilynetwork )

5. We would like to use some photos of the party on our website and VFN Facebook pages.
