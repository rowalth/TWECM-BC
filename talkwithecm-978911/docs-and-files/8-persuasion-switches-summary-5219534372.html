8 Persuasion Switches - Summary


1. THE SWITCH: PRESTIGE 

HOW TO USE IT:
Use it as a HOOK for your sales page
 Use it to open your sales video
 Use it to introduce your offer in a webinar
 Use it to craft your bio/elevator pitch for podcasts/interviews Use it when introducing yourself on social media 

EXAMPLE FROM MY FILES:
How to get the woman of your dreams without worrying about rejection or insult 
How to be the hottest mom in your circle without resorting to starvation or eating boiled veggies for the rest of your life 

ACTIVATOR PHRASES:
How to <insert a prestige booster> without <insert a prestige diminisher>

2. THE SWITCH: BELIEVABILITY 

HOW TO USE IT:
Use it to build the case for your product or your expertise: - In a sales page after the offer
 - Throughout your webinar
 - To open sales emails 
- To open sales videos 

EXAMPLE FROM MY FILES:
Now you can write high converting copy EVEN IF you can barely write a grocery list
 This works even better if you know nothing about sales 

ACTIVATOR PHRASES:
Yes you can <insert results they crave> even if <the mental block they have about why they can’t get those results> 

3. THE SWITCH: PARITY 

HOW TO USE IT:
Use it to show your point of differentiation compared to other alternatives:
- In a table on sales page
 - As a social media post 
- In webinar pitch
 - In launch emails
 - In sales calls
 - During interviews 

EXAMPLE FROM MY FILES:
Here are 10 ways Sold Out Launch is radically different from everything out there
 I know what you are thinking...”NOT another launch program” Let me tell you why this isn’t YET another launch program... 

ACTIVATOR PHRASES:
Would you rather <insert the alternative> or would you rather <insert your point of differentiation>
 Wondering how this is different from the gazillion other solutions out there? Here’s how <insert a comparative table> 


4. THE SWITCH: DESIRABILITY
HOW TO USE IT:
Use it to paint an after picture with your solution. In a sales page after introducing your offer. In a webinar pitch right before you are about to close. To conclude sales video. As a social media post 

EXAMPLE FROM MY FILES:
Imagine this day with me...
 You wake up in the morning, not to refund requests or “lost password” emails but to 11 new sales you made while you slept...all from a persuasive sales page you put together in less than an hour the night before...
 As you sip your coffee you type out an email that takes you 10 minutes to pull together and BOOM, your cash-generating, revenue- inducing email is on it’s merry way to make you tons of money....and you don’t even have to worry about the “right words”
 You decide to take the rest of the day off...because you can and because the hundreds of hours you had to spend writing copy, tweaking copy and slowly killing all your brain cells are now FREE... spa day seems like a great idea! 

ACTIVATOR PHRASES:
Imagine how It’s <one year from the date> and you are <paint a picture of the life AFTER” 


5. THE SWITCH: CURIOSITY
HOW TO USE IT:
Use it to get people to open, click or scroll; Emails, Social media posts, FB ads, Sales page/ optin page openers, Sales video openers, To transition from teaching to pitch in a webinar 

EXAMPLE FROM MY FILES:
Can a 100 pound girl defeat a heavyweight boxing champion in a fist fight?
Nothing like the smell of piss & horseshit to start the day! 

ACTIVATOR PHRASES: 
Ever wonder how <insert something unlikely>?
 Want to see me <insert something goofy>?
THIS is how <insert something they really want to know but is eluding them> 


6. THE SWITCH: URGENCY
HOW TO USE IT:
Use it to get people to act right NOW. On sales pages. On launch emails. In live webinar pitches. In “closing now” FB ads 

EXAMPLE FROM MY FILES:
Wake up Dude, EPS Train is leaving the station... It’s now or never time, it’s do or die time 

ACTIVATOR PHRASES: 
Tick tock <insert time left>
 Wake up dude <insert quantity left>
 Your last chance to <get the result they crave> in 2016 goes away in <time left to cart close> 

7. THE SWITCH: RELATABILITY
HOW TO USE IT:
Use it to make a personal connection with the prospective buyer In marketing videos. In webinar opener. In interviews/podcasts. In social media posts. In emails 

EXAMPLE FROM MY FILES:
I am not perfect...not even close
 I send emails to clients with glaring typos ( boobs instead of books) My presentations are off.
 My headers are skewed.
 My cover images are done in canva (and look the part)
 My videos have so many ummms I want to die when I re-watch or try to edit (and that’s why I don’t edit)
 My blogs are riddled with so many spelling mistakes; it would embarrass my 8 year old
 I routinely put broken or wrong links in my newsletters
 I am not perfect.
I am just good enough...and that’s good enough for me. 

ACTIVATOR PHRASES:
I wasn’t always like this <insert story of your humble beginnings>
 You might not know this about me but <insert some weird fact about you> I am just like you <insert some aspect of your life they can relate to> You may look at me and think I have everything together but the reality is a bit different <insert your struggles> 


8. THE SWITCH: EDUTAINMENT
HOW TO USE IT:
Use it to keep them hooked & entertained as they stay & read through.
 Throughout webinars
 In social media posts 
In marketing videos
In emails
On sales page to keep people engaged 

EXAMPLE FROM MY FILES:
What if pigs actually could eat (and digest) chocolate Nutella REALLY is thicker than water
 For the love of cookies
 I woke up at the Buttcrack of dawn 

ACTIVATOR PHRASES:
I have to tell you about that one time <insert a relevant anecdote> 
A <relevant character> and <relevant character> and <relevant character> want in a bar... 
10 ways <insert a funny analysis of your industry>
