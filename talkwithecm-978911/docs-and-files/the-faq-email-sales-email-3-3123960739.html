The FAQ Email: Sales Email #3

Note from Raelyn: I used to think FAQ emails were completely lame and do not work, until one day I was on a discovery call with a prospective high-end private client.

We were just about to end the call, and I directed her to my sales page to make the payment.

To my surprise, she started reading every single FAQ I had on my sales page out loud to me on the phone, before saying that she was buying! So people actually read the FAQs!
