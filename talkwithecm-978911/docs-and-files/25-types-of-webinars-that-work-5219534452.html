25 Types of Webinars - that work!

https://kartrausers.s3.amazonaws.com/GenesisDigital/8694913_15852472281016_Fast_Ways_Webinar_can_grow_your_business.pdf

Meaningful engagement from YOU (Your company)
  1. random acts of kindness
  2. Did you know? (addressing objections - did you know you can have YouTube success on youtube WITHOUT a camera??
    Another Did You Know topic could be “Why you don’t even need a video camera to have great success with YouTube.” You then go on to explain how Powerpoint slides, screen captures, mind maps, and flow charts make excellent tutorial and training videos for YouTube consumption. This obviously overcomes a huge objection – I hate being on camera – and by offering this option, your attendee will be far more open to considering YouTube as a traffic source. 

  3. Demonstration 
  4. Success tips from BIG shots - celebrity power 
  5. Product/bonus delivery - LIVE class/consultation
  6. Customer follow up - multiple LIVE touches
  7. The deep dive
  8. Top questions
  9. Success stories - Zero to Hero
  10. What's Happening NOW
  11. The best of...review of your material 
  12. Product review - another product that is potentially valuable to your audience
  13. Fortune Teller? Predict the future 
  14. What I learned from (success or failure or experience)
  15. My wikipedia - dictionary of insiders terms & abbrivations
  16. Expert interview - ask 5 questions as if you were a beginner
  17. Resource Guide
  18. Free resource guide
  19. survey results
  20. FAQ - look for q's with complicated or difficult answers = better content = more youtube traffic
  21. share reader responses - comments
  22. behind the curtain - a day in the life, habits, quirks, methods, etc. 
  23. They did WHAT? - strange/unique/unusual ways people are using your tools
  24. above & beyond - next step (after successful) 

    • If you teach Business Growth, you might explore Tax Planning Strategies. 


    • If you teach Exercise, you might explore Injury Recovery 


    • If you teach Guitar Lessons, you might explore Audio Engineering. 


    • If you teach Child-Rearing, you might explore having a better relationship with your parents. 


    • If you teach resume writing, you might explore negotiating salary. 


    • If you teach Video Marketing, you might explore Webinars. 

  25. Calling BS - Causing trouble - Taking an unpopular stand! (be authentic!)
