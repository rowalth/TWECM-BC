book cover - copy sells, story

Cover attracts, copy sells.
A friend of mine recently learned this hard lesson about sales shortly after releasing her first book. She had a beautiful cover. She had powerful images to attract, the right colors to create a mood, everything fit. But after it debuted on the best-seller list it quickly sank. Sales were plummeting...but she wasn't sure why. It was getting great reviews. The people who read it - LOVED it, but...why weren't more people buying?

The reality of verbal branding:
There is no funnel, no strategy, no sales video, no ad strategy - nothing that is going to make a bad message effective!

So, what is your core message? This is your foundation and will determine what your path to success will look like. Is it going to be a long track up to the peak of a mountain, an uphill battle in the snow, sleet and rain? Or will it be more like a walk down to the beach, listening to your favorite tunes along the way?

Sometimes it really is as simple as that. Are people responding to your message with likes, comments, shares, screaming "YES - I need this!" and throwing their wallets at you? Or are you starting at an empty bank account and wondering why people just aren't buying? 

If you sell something people don't need....it's hard! You'll spend way to much time trying to convince people, begging people to just try your product. No one wants to do that.
