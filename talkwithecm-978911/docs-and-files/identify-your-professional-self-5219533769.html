Identify your professional self!

Reflect on your past experience and respond with keywords only:

1. Have you ever discovered a problem & then worked to solve it? What was the result?

2. Have you ever introduced a new system or workflow that has made it easier for you or others to work or improve outcomes?

3. Did you save time and money for the company? How much? What positive effects did these savings have on the company?
 
4. Have your efforts increased the size of the company? When?

5. Have you recovered productivity, idling or reduced waste? What were the effects?

6. Think of examples of effective project or personnel management. What was the result of your efforts?

7. Did you initiate a functioning sales program or another large project? What was the quantitative result? How did it affect the image of the company or the motivation of the employees?

8. Were you involved in decision-making processes or planning? What contribution have you made to this? What were the results of your efforts?

9. Did you publish important reports, research contributions or newspaper articles? Who was the target audience? How big & long was the reach?

10. Have you increased the efficiency of employees or work processes? How high are the savings?

11. Were you responsible for reducing staff or reducing the incidence of work? How did you manage these tasks?

12. Were you involved in foundations or closures? What challenges did you encounter? What were your successes?

13. Have you developed new work systems or processes? How many people did you train or support in using the new system? How much money did these changes provide?

14. Have you made connections between individual departments? How have you managed to increase your efficiency?

15. Have you been able to produce reports or statistics that have better informed operators and thus facilitated decision-making?

16. Do you receive any awards, recognitions or special engagements? If so, why?

17. Has someone in a higher position noted your achievement or complimented you on your contributions??
