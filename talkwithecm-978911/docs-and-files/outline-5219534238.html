Be Memorable and leave a lasting impression
Something we ALL have in common - you entered this room!
What did you notice? Who caught your attention. make a mental note of that, we'll come back to it later. 
The thing is that living in a city, we see people ALL the time, so in a way each & everyone of you is an expert at reading people & deciding who is memorable or not. 

Let's take another example . the Wiener Linien (ubahn, bus, tram, train)
Ubahn example:
every one is looking down - at the paper, at their phone - Here in vienna there are even magazines available, if you forgot to bring something to look at. Mainly people tend to keep to themselves & mind their own business. Which is pretty impressive on the around 6pm!

Now our focus tonight is to talk about the memorable people.
