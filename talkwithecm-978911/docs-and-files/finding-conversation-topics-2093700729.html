finding conversation topics

Hello Islam! 

Thanks for your question 
It's a really good question and a topic that many people struggle with - conversation topics!

Maybe you're sitting around the table, waiting for the meeting to start...but you don't know what to talk about.

Or you join your colleagues for an after work party...but you're not sure how to strike up a conversation outside of work topics.

Or perhaps small talk is not a problem, but you want to move beyond meaningless chit-chat, connect on a personal level and add real value!

So, you are looking for advanced topics to start conversations with your colleagues - this is a good question, because you are very specific about the exact context - your colleagues, or people that you see often. But before we start jumping into solutions...I'd want to dig a little deeper, to help get a clearer image of the exact situation. Here are a few questions to help:

- What are you currently doing to start conversations with your colleagues? 
- How do you feel in that exact moment? 

Knowing these answers will help zero in on the best (& most sustainable!) solution for you!

To get really great conversation topics for a specific group of people, it is important to do a bit of pre-work. That is, a bit a research to set yourself up BEFORE you even start speaking. From your question, I know that you are ready to take that step. 

Here is a 3 part technique to really listen in an connect with others that I use, my clients are using and I recommend for you:

1. Listen - Before jumping in and trying to be the center of attention, take some time to sit back and listen. What are people talking about? Which topics are interesting / funny / important to this specific group of people? Pay attention and take notes.

2. Ask questions - Focus on open ended questions, especially HOW & WHY questions. Ask these questions to pull out the story of others and get them to talk more. A great tip to start is to prepare a few interesting questions in advance.
 
 3. Experiment - So now that you know topics that this specific group likes to discuss, start talking about it! If you’re in a group of people that loves talking about food - bring up that new restaurant across town, or the Jamie Oliver’s recent tweet about meat free meals! 
 
 The most important thing at each step is to pay close attention to how people respond (write it down!), reflect and try again :)
