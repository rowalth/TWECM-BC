3 Types of audience

taken from socialtriggers.tv (https://socialtriggers.com/how-to-write-headlines/)

The Informed (smallest segment of customers):
They know already everything and want to be convinced by features. E.g. the 5 blade razer is better than the 3 blade razer.

for TWECM:
  • learn english speaking on the go (feature mania - accessible on mobile devices)
  • proven system to improve spoken english (xxx people already were successful)

The Afflicted (segment to scale your business):
These are the people who are looking for answers. To get their attention start with the problem. They have a solvable problem but don't know how to solve it yet. These people are looking for answers on google with specific questions.

for TWECM:
  • we show you a specific path to improve your spoken English
  • TWECM takes away the fear to talk
  • We organize the people for you, so you don't have to

The Oblivious (largest segment of customers):
These are people who have a problem, but they do not know that they can solve the problem / sometimes they don't even know they have the problem. You can't assume people know what you know. To get their attention

for TWECM:
  • to increase your salary we'll improve your skills (talking english)
  • we have the key to an international career (talking english confidently)
