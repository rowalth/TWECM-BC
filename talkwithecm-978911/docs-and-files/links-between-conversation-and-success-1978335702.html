links between conversation and success

If you’re reading this right now, I know that you, like me, are sick and tired of coming up short in conversations.
And maybe you don’t know why…

You’ve probably believed that the key to happiness and success is based on how hard you work…(that’s what I believed my whole life)

I busted my butt in grad school, had 10x more data and research than any of my classmates when I presented my PhD thesis proposal, yet I was the one getting passed over for every career defining opportunity.

The truth is, you are missing out on opportunities because of a lie you have been told your entire life...
HARD WORK = SUCCESS
WRONG
It's the most common lie ever told.
The REAL key to success? 
Relationships
And what leads to relationships...ConversationsNot just ANY conversation...Charismatic, Connected, Meaningful Conversations
(which most of us aren't having)
Do any of these situations sound familiar?
  • You hesitate to walk up to someone out of fear of rejection.
  • ​You find yourself in the corner looking at your phone at a social events because it’s easier than talking to strangers.
  • ​You avoid approaching people because you fear the conversation will end awkwardly.
  • You avoid conversation altogether because you're sick of meaningless small talk.
  • You feel like life-changing opportunities are passing you by.
  • You stay quiet in big meetings at work, even when you have the answers.
  • You fantasize about a stunner at the store that you couldn't muster the courage to approach.
  • You see a business hero of yours at a networking event but freeze up and can't think of anything to say.



Meaningful relationships are built through conversation
Living in a tech driven world has suffocated us from developing in-person communication skills…

We dread showing up on first dates, luncheons, or networking events because NO ONE knows how to genuinely connect with each other…

It’s an endless small-talk loop...No thanks!

I’d rather stay home and binge watch Game of Thrones, wouldn't you? 

It's like we are all going through the motions with smartphones in hand.

But, what if I told you that you could learn how to have winning conversations & build deeper connections professionally, socially, & romantically? 

(without using any sleazy manipulation or persuasion techniques)
Over the last twelve years we have distilled the secret to successful relationships... they all begin with Charismatic Conversations.
At the end of the day, relationships are everything. And the ability to form stronger relationships can be taught.
It doesn’t matter what area of life you want to succeed in, either…
Romance? It will come from your relationships...
Business? It will come from your relationships…
Happiness? It will come from your relationships...

45% of promotions & hires are from employee referrals. 

68% of all romances (from one-night stands to marriages) are from introductions by mutual friends. 

So, what is the QUICKEST and MOST EFFECTIVE path to building relationships? 

It’s NOT Tinder…
It’s NOT LinkedIn...
It’s NOT Social Media…

It is... 
In-person conversations.
Conversations are not going away.

We need to be able to communicate in order to connect with others.
Can you afford to continue making meaningless small talk that does nothing to grow your network or social circle?
