SYCWS - feedback

Takeaways week 1: 

  • Get to know your inner Critic- It´s important to recognize the messages that come from    your Inner Critic to change them. 
  • After recognizing the messages you must change your responses and listen to your Inner Coach.
  • I realized that this is a long process but we have to practice hardly and take action every time you listen your Inner Critic.  
  • After this week I could recognize my messages that come from my Inner Critic and which situations that they come in my mind. I could also learn how I change my thoughts listening my Inner Coach. 
Takeaways week 2: 

  • This week I learned how to tell my story, how to build my massage and make it irresistible. The first step is setting what value, which massage I want to show to my listeners and trough my story I can make it powerful. The most important takeaway this week is “embrace your struggles” when you tell your own story and experiences. 

Takeaways week 3: 

  • How important is being prepared to deliver your massage in even the most challenging situations. I learned you really have to prepare what you are going to say and who is your audience to engaging others. Being prepared in challenging situations (networking event, interviews..) makes totally difference to reach your goal. 

Takeaway week 4: 

  • With the insights and tools that I´ve learned with Elizabeth, I feel more confident and comfortable to face challenging situations. I learned that being prepared makes huge difference to engage others and deliver your message successfully and I leaned tools how to be prepared.  Through this course, I developed skills to keep moving forward and enhance my confidence to face situations and experiences that I feel out my comfort zone.
