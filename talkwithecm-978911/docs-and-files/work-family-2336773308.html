Many working moms have told me that the question inevitably comes up, "Do you really want to work full time?" as if there is something wrong with even considering this option. And working papas have said that they feel frustrated when people aren't taking their choice seriously, assuming that papas can't really be solely responsible for childcare, "Can't their mom watch them?" Wether these questions come from friends, in-laws or co-workers, they still hit home drawing our decisions into question. 


As working parents in Vienna, there are a lot of rights and benefits available to help support us. The exact rules may not always be clear, especially for people who just moved here, and it may feel like you are pushed in one direction or the other, but the truth is that we do have a choice. A big choice: Full time vs. Part time.


Although it may sound like a simple yes or no question, there is really a lot behind this question. What does it mean to work full time? What are the real costs? What about part time? 


Of course many people reflect on and talk about financial impacts when considering this question: cash flow, standard of living, child care costs, retirement savings, etc. But what else is at stake here? What does it really mean to share the load?


Practically, financially & emotionally - What is your experience?


Our panelists will open up and share about their experiences and decision making process with us.
