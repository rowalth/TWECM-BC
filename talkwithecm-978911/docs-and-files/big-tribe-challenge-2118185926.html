    BUILDER:     If this is your top marketing style, then creating and sharing tools, ideas, and platforms is the best way to attract and nurture your prospects and turn them into clients.


    CONNECTOR:     If this is your top marketing style, then connecting with people and becoming a "hub" for your target audience must be present in some shape or form in every marketing activity you choose to communicate your highest value - from attracting prospects to enrolling clients.


    CHAMPION:     Business owners with this marketing style must focus on inspiring potential clients through building a personal brand, sharing their message, becoming a visionary and a leader of their tribe, and inviting others to support their mission.
