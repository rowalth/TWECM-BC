January Meeting - We kicked off the new quarter, new year & new decade with a new theme - all about adding VALUE! 
  • Workshop - The 3 Things You Need To Know To Introduce Yourself Without Feeling Awkward by Elizabeth from talkwithecm
  • Location - The Collabratory 
  • Community Dinner - Chillax
People came to the workshop with different goals and ideas. We focused in on starting conversations and introducing yourself to someone new. This workshop was all about zeroing in on the essential elements of reaching out, connecting with others and networking to get the results you want. We pulled back the curtain on how to approach new people, including exactly what to say and do to keep the conversation flowing in a direction that you want. 

Some of the most remarkable work happened as we started sharing personal stories & developing our own introductions! Here's what people were saying after the workshop:
  • I have some tools-questions so that I am prepared for a discussion.
  • I gained a lot of insights about eye contact, preparation for the event, & how to take a fresh look at my own posture.
  • The next time I'm at a party, or work event I know exactly how to pick a group to join!
  • In a short amount of time, we really learned a few efficient and important social skills. 
I closed the workshop by giving everyone a free gift- My Super Savvy Introduction Guide, packed with everything you need to know to introduce yourself to anyone without feeling awkward. The meeting ended with open discussions and the group transitioned to a cozy, awkward-free dinner at Chillax!
