Conquering Your Inner CriticYour Challenge for Week 1
Everyone has an Inner Critic, and each one is as unique and variable as we are. When developing professionally, it is not uncommon to hear nagging self-doubts such as,
Why would anyone listen to me? What if I don't have the right credentials? or I don't have anything new to offer.
The truth is that you do have something of value to offer and people want it! This is especially true when you are preparing for a job interview. There are some things people just can't see until you show them:your eduction / experience
your hard work to get where you are today
your kind / funny personality
