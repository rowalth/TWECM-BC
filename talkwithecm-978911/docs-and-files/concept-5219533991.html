Concept

teaching the teachers
not a one-time input, those are great!, but we will take your hand & go the journey with you

mastermind concept - you can see & do more (achieve more!) in a qualified group

Of course you don't have to join this group. You can go out, develop your skills & become a great teacher without this group, many people have. But this group offers you 
  • great resources
  • teaching  breakdowns
  • worksheets
  • clear templates
  • actual scrips
And as a bonus, in the live sessions you have access to our feedback rounds: where we  bring the actual challenges and situations you face to practice in real time and get tactical input and real solutions for moving forward


There are plenty of teachers out there who are not part of group like this
do you need this class to be a great teacher? No, but imagine how much better you could be, how much more you can grow, with this foundation, starting at this base.

it takes time & effort to get good at something. If you want to really get good & excel at what you do, most people hire a professional to show them how - learning to play the violin, or a personal training. Since you are reading this post, Just stop and think about how much time & money you put into learning how to dance! Why wouldn't you want do the same for your teaching? 

If you don't, someone else will! 
You'll have better retention rates, people will keep coming back for more, you can name your price &, best of all, you'll be earning money doing what you love!
