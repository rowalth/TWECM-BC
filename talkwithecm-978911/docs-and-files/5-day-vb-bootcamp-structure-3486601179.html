5 day VB bootcamp structure

*Pre-start: Get to know each other - tell me about you / your business / who you are & why you are here. What do you hope to achieve / gain from this bootcamp?

  1. Foundation = Start with what you have: core elements (who/what/how) [30 second pitch] - answers the why you question?
  2. What is verbal branding: 3 levels = the heart / mind / soul of your brand
    1. heart - values, what it loves, what it hates, who your brand actually is at the core
    2. mind - the story & purpose, framework for viewing & interacting with the world
      • take agency and tell your story in a meaningful way
      • Can’t control what happens to us but can control the story we tell
      • Why/what you’re doing- meaningful story = purpose, answers the why me question & connects on a deeper level
    3. soul - the exact words / language & feeling of your brand
  3. Primary goal - of a personal brand, a company brand, any brand at all is connecting with your audience, the exact people you want to notice you (your product/service), fall in love, etc. & starting - building - strong relationships. Relationships based on trust. But how to start?? How to get that first touch - without being creepy?? Sparking interest - agility & Super Savvy Introduction
  4. The engagement - how to get those conversations flowing? [conversation playbook]
    1. copy vs. content
      meant for people to take action vs. educate
    2. Imposters syndrome - it's Not about YOU
      1. +grammatical errors
        +typos
        +spelling mistakes

        --selling the emotion
        --appealing to the logic, why should they buy/ work with you

        STOP brushing it outside, NO OUTSOURCING! 
        never under estimate the value of your words:
      • emails
      • sales requests
      • landing pages
      • social posts
    3. I will show you how you can master the art of copy writing, like no one has ever done before! I'm talking about taking back the full control of your words, how people see you & how they feel about you.

    4. I want you to be in control of your copy writing - your branding - your words - you can do this!


  5. Scaling & Expanding your reach, even during a pandemic - How to talk to anyone guide
