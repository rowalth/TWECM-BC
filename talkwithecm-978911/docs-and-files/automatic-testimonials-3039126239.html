Hi Name


I wanted to thank you your business! It was a pleasure to read that people appreciate the hard work we are doing!


I was wondering if you'd be interested in the following proposal.


We would like to ask you to record a video testimonial which would go on our website (in the testimonials section). For your trouble, we'd like to give you a $100 TFX gift certificate.


I feel that people would respond really well to genuine reviews from real clients - which is why we're reaching to you personally.


A few logistical points about it:


  • Doesn't have to be long - perhaps 1 minute or shorter

  • Please do it in a well-lit environment (preferably during the day). Outside would also work if it's not windy.

  • You can record with your laptop or phone.

  • You can upload to YouTube or send us the video file

A few things to mention that I think would make your video great
  • You only have to give your first name

  • What country you live in (or if you don't want to provide the country - which continent)

  • What is your profession (i.e. "I'm a doctor" or "I'm a teacher")

  • When did you start using TFX (i.e. "I've been using TFX for 3 years")

  • Your overall thoughts about the service (here you get to say whatever you want :) ) 

  • Is there one thing you really like that you think sets us apart?

  • Would you recommend us to others?



Overall I think it would probably take you 5 minutes or less to record - not a bad way to bank $100 as well as barrels of gratitude from yours truly!
