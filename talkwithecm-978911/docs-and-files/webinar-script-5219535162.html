ecm - to all of this I  want to add the Why - The big WHY, not the what or the how, we have two highly accomplished and experienced experts are providing those answers for you - easy access right now to an entire framework of support - with a free consultation! It's really a no-brainer - do it!

My question for you tonight is - WHY are you doing what you do? 
Gives you clarity & your source of Motivation, focus & perseverance

Rather than getting bogged down with the demands & tasks of life, it is crucial to zero in on your big why, why are you here? Why are you doing this? I can tell you it’s NOT the money. Without even knowing a person, I can tell you that’s never the why. That will just fizzle out quickly. I’ve seen in my own life that once I can get crystal clear about my why, the  energy, the motivation, the resources fall into place, that’s all just details - everything comes together. Without asking directly, after hearing the stories & accomplishments of our guest speakers today, I am willing to bet crazy amounts of money that they are totally clear about their big why - their focal point in life. 

Another key question that helps me get to my BIG WHY - What’s in it for you & your family? What are the Benefits? 
-> Try to complete this sentence: I'm doing this because... I'm so glad that I started this group / volunteering / getting my certification / doing whatever it is your are thinking about taking on... a year ago because...
& if the answers aren't inspiring,
