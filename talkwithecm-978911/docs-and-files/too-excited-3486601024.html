too excited??

Selling is NOT manipulating.
If you have a product - a good product - something that you really believe in - something that you know will actually help people & make their lives/ their businesses / their relationships / whatever better -
It is YOUR responsibility to share it! Get it out there & make it accessible for others.  

I often get shamed about being too excited when I am selling.

"Why are you shouting?"
"What’s with all the giggles?"
"Why are you acting as if you have won the lottery?"

I even had someone say to me on a live webinar:

"No one wants to see your tatas bouncing all over the screen, calm the F down"

So I bounced some more because "come on Karen, we all know you came for the tatas doing the cha cha"

Here's what those shamers don't know.

I sell so much because of this very thing.
My unabashed enthusiasm, my mad excitement, a sort of giddy joy interlaced with shouts of YES and OMGs.

Because if you can't exhibit that kind of zeal for your own offer, what makes you think your buyer will?
And it’s simple, just bring full-on high energy when you are selling.

Remember, people buy your conviction, your enthusiasm, your confidence in your offer BEFORE they buy your offer. If you are like "meh whatever", guess what they think too?
