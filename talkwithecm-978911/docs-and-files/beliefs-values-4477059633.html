Beliefs/Values

American songwriter and activist Meek Mill once said, “Just build your brand from day one, man. Your brand is your name, basically. A lot of people don't know that they need to build their brand, your brand is what keeps you moving.”

And I couldn’t agree more. But what is my brand? It’s my love of lettuce, meat pie etc.) and my dislike of chocolate, croissants etc.)

My brand is the pleasure I get from books, old stamps etc.) rather than diamonds, dresses, shoes etc.)

My brand is my determination to be, and to surround myself with people who believe in integrity, equality, justice for all etc.)

My brand is my passion in helping to feeding & educating children, supporting small business owners etc. 

What is YOUR brand?
**
French poet Anatole France said, “To accomplish great things, we must not only act, but also dream; not only plan, but also believe.”

My big dream is change the lives of a million people struggling with anxiety etc.. But I’m determined to do that with integrity, equality, justice for all etc.)

Step one of my plan is to get over my procrastination, inner chatter, overwhelm, old belief systems etc.)

That’s easier said than done, isn’t it? How did you deal with your motivational struggles?
**
If I could only be teaching history in Scotland, Travelling with the circus etc.) I would feel as free as a bird.

No boundaries.
No limits.
No commitments.

How perfect would that be?

What would YOU do if time and money were no object?
**
If there’s one thing that makes my heart soar, it’s knowing that I can contribute towards feeding & educating children, supporting small business owners etc. 

You may underestimate your own capability to help others, but you must trust in your ability to make an impact. 

American author Byron Katie once said, “I believe in the power of every human being to end suffering”.

I make sure to integrate this cause into my life, and if my business can allow me to make one small change, then I feel like my job is done and my life feels that much brighter.

What cause brings joy to your heart?

**

Good values are:

Uplifting.
Inspiring.
Life changing.

I believe that values are what makes life worthwhile. They bond us together in humanity, and they push us to continue to do better.

For me, a non-negotiable moral value that is part of my life code is integrity, equality, justice for all etc.) and I make sure to incorporate that in every aspect of my living, including my business.

I am convinced that your values bring a positive energy to your work, which radiates towards all those around you.

What is the core value that you choose to live by?
**
Bringing in the dollars is awesome, but what truly gives me so much joy is teaching history in Scotland, Travelling with the circus etc.)

And having MY business means that I can choose to do this, all the while maintaining my feeding & educating children, supporting small business owners etc.

And this means more to me than all the riches in the world.

(well, not ALL the riches ;) )

Or to discover that I’d much rather be taking a nap, reading a book etc.) than partying till midnight, doing a backyard BBQ etc.)
But that’s what makes ME uniquely me.
