Navigating the uncharted waters of the working parent
Hello ,

What? How? WHY?!?There are thousands of questions we face as working parents and although it may seem that everyone is quick to point out what we are doing wrong, the last time I checked there is no definitive set of rules to guide the way.This month Suzanne, one of our own members, will share her personal story about navigating her way through the uncharted waters of becoming a working parent. She'll open up about 'mom guilt' and how she determined the right time to return to work, combined with the logistics of pumping milk, organising daycare, syncing schedules & more!

Like other VFN meetings, this event is an excellent opportunity for you to see familiar faces, meet new friends and get inspired by the stories of other members.

This meeting is for all VFN Professionals.
