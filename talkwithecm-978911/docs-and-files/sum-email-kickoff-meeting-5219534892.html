DRAFT 2:
Hello (name),

Even though our first meeting was already a week ago, I still get a huge smile on my face when I think about it. I really appreciated the opportunity to meet together face to face and hear some of your ideas about how we want to shape this group and move forward.

I'm writing this email to sum up what happened and give clear steps for what's next.

What happened?
The VFN Professionals KICKOFF Meeting, our first meeting, was held on Wednesday, August 29, 2018. We started things in a relaxed way at 18:30 in an inner courtyard with lots of plants and a mini water fountain off to the side. We got to know each other with a meet & greet gathering and some Bingo fun. At 19:00 we moved inside to the colorful meeting room for the more formal portion of the evening. Here, we addressed the questions:
  • Who are we?
We each introduced ourselves and Roman shared a personal story.
  • What do we want to do?
Elizabeth briefly described her vision for VFN Professionals followed by a group brainstorming session. We asked the question: How do we want our meetings to look like? What do we want from this group?

We ended the evening with a community dinner at a nearby Asian noodle restaurant. I love eating international food with an international crowd! (Sorry, no photos.)

What about the prizes?
Of course, I can't forget to mention our prize winners! Congratulations to our Bingo winners: Angela, Sophie & Oana, and our brainstorming team winners - with 22 ideas!

A great big 'YES!'
Personally speaking, this meeting was a huge affirmation, a great big 'YES!' that we are moving and growing in a way that matters to you, our members. In fact, this is what I dreamed of when I first started the group - VFN Professionals LIVE meetings offering:
  • content with real value, 
  • the opportunity to connect with experienced professionals, 
  • new ideas and perspectives,
  • positive energy to boost confidence and skills
  • and the chance for you to give back to your community too!

What are others saying about the event?
  • Oana P. It was simply great, thanks to you and your husband for organizing! I enjoyed meeting everyone face-to-face and to play the introduction game, was fun to do it that way. I especially appreciated listening to a man's perspective on work life balance. More "success stories" or personal examples like this would be wonderful! It's simply an amazing international group :)
  • Sophie L. Really enjoyed it!!! I appreciate not only the prospect of lots of helpful meetings and workshop in the future, but also the social aspect of it all. Meetings like this should definitely take place regularly. Thanks so much for the initiative !! üëçüí™üòé
  • Claudia C. Lots of positive energy, many ideas to build on, great advices from direct experience, fun, Thanks Elizabeth and Roman!

So, what's next?
Now is the exciting part - it is time to plan our next meeting! We already build up a framework and have a lot of momentum. As a group, let's pull together to make it work & keep it running. If each person contributes one small piece, we can collectively accomplish great things!

How do you want to be involved?
  • Do you like taking pictures? We are looking for people to help document our meetings & events.
  •  Do you like design? We'd like an image/logo that better represents us for the top of this email.
  • Do you have something to offer for our next meeting? A personal story, skills sharing & tips are all welcome!
  • Do you have another idea? Welcome!
Write me an email and let me know how you can contribute to our next meeting. 

Thanks in advance for your contributions,

Hello (name),

The kickoff meeting of VFN Professionals was a huge success! I am so thankful that we took the chance to jump out of the digital world, put away our screens and meet together face to face. 

Personally speaking, this meeting was a huge affirmation, a great big 'YES!' that we are moving and growing in a way that matters to you, our members. In fact, this is what I dreamed of when I first started the group - VFN Professionals LIVE meetings offering 
  • content with real value, 
  • the opportunity to connect with experienced professionals, 
  • new ideas and perspectives,
  • positive energy to boost confidence and skills
  • and the chance for you to give back to your community too!

As one of our members, Edicta, pointed out, "The more we share the good stuff, the more of it there is!" So in this email, I want to express my thanks and share some of the 'good stuff' from our meeting with you.

First of all, we would like to thank M.U.T. for the great location. Located near the Naschmarkt, this venue is the home of a socially minded organisation that works to help meet the immediate needs of people, the environment and animals. The space was open and comfortable with sofas for lounging and lots of plants.

We also want to thank Soba Noodles for working together with our time schedule and giving us a welcoming space for our community dinner. I always love eating international food with an international crowd.

Next we want to thank the VFN, no only for helping us as parents, but for giving us the platform to meet together and create something new as professionals. In fact, without this organisation there would be no VFN Professionals!

Last, but certainly not least, we want to thank you - the members of VFN Professionals. During the meeting, we took a moment to look around and see the real faces of VFN Professionals. This was a powerful moment. As a group we represent many nations, cultures and industries. We are freelancers, employees, CEOs, business owners and more. We are parents who want to make the best life that we can for our children. We bring a wide range of knowledge and experience to the group. Celebrating the unity in our diversity, we thank you for believing in us.
