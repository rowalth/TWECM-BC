Meeting Summary

Hello everyone!

Moving right along! Our October meeting had a relaxed and intimate vibe. I really appreciated the opportunity to speak with some of you about your ideas and how we want to continue shaping this group and moving forward. 

This summary email is for all who participated and also everyone who couldn't join us but still wants to stay on top of what's going on. I also want to get you excited for our November meeting!

What happened:

NOTE: You can read this grey section for the details of the meeting or skip ahead to the next white section.

We started the meeting in the courtyard with a casual meet & greet and some light refreshments, including a delicious fruit cake - Sensational! Thanks Oana! It was nice to see everyone networking and sharing stories. 

At 19:00 we moved inside to start the formal part of our meeting with a personal story from one of our members. Simona shared her personal journey followed by a Q&A session. She highlighted key lessons which included:
  • being aware that professional goals change over time as children become older
  • the necessity of clear communication about needs with employers
  • the working cultures in different major cities (London & Vienna)
  • the overall impact on her ideal job finding process
Thanks Simona for sharing these insightful inputs with our group.

In small groups we moved on to familiarise ourselves with the brainstorming ideas from the first meeting as well as add on a few more ideas. We agreed to prioritise and vote on the collected ideas online --> This information will be sent out in a separate email, so stay tuned!

To round off the evening, Roman briefly shared about a structured approach for achieving focused work in a distracted world. The essence of this approach is to get a pile of work done in an environment that enables peak performance --> a mix of social accountability and lightweight structure.  

As promised, here is the link where you and your spouse can sign up to get more information and join his next session: https://go.talkwithecm.com/productivity

At the community dinner, we enjoyed some more time to kickback and continue connecting & sharing together. 

I am very pleased to announce that the volunteer aspect of our group is shaping up - there are a lot of people that I'd like to thank this month. Your contributions are exactly what make this group possible.
Special thanks to:
Kathrin - for jumping in last minute to help our set up crew
Oana - for the delicious cake!
Alicia & Oana - our fabulous photo team
Simona - for sharing her personal story

Have a great weekend!
Elizabeth & Roman
