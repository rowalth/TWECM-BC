1. Welcome to our tribe :)
Hello !

Welcome to VFN Professionals!

Welcome to our growing group - I am glad that you are here. VFN Professionals is a solutions oriented group focused on questions around the topic "How can I be seen as a professional and have a family at the same time?" 

Our activities are based on three core values: 
  1. Connect
  2. Reflect
  3. Perfect

These three words describe the active role members take and the opportunities available to you within our group.

This group makes it easy for you to connect with others who already understand the challenges you face as both a professional and an international family living here in Vienna.

Often when facing struggles, I feel alone, as if I'm the only person who has ever encountered this situation. As I open up and share with others, I have learned that like me, a lot of people feel isolated confused and don't even know where to find support. I used to think that if I was smarter or more experienced, I wouldn't have these problems. The truth is that I've seen even highly skilled professional people feel stuck and frustrated, desperately looking for solutions. I created this group so we can all join together, share information & find support. Some people join this group because they want to save their career, others want to bring harmony to their family, for others the priority now is to resurrect their social life and for quite a number of people they want to tackle all of these areas (yikes!).

At VFN Professionals we understand what you are going through because many of us have been there and have already faced the same challenges. By discussing and walking through topics with us, you too can get unstuck and find real solutions. As a group we can share resources and support from experience and insider knowledge.

You are not alone, you have found your tribe.

Now that you know a bit more about our group, take a moment to think about others you know who could benefit as well. By inviting others to this group, you can help them connect and find solutions as well as enrich your own experience with the group. As one member wrote, "The more we share the good stuff, the more of it there is!"

Are you excited? We are. Because we believe everyone can benefit from sharing our collective knowledge and experience. Invite friends and colleagues to join you in the group with a message like this:

❝I just joined VFN Professionals. This is a solutions oriented group focused on questions around the topic "How can I be seen as a professional and have a family at the same time?" Join me! http://go.talkwithecm.com/vfn-professionals❞

Thanks for helping us help others. Watch your inbox for news about updates & meetings.

I look forward to meeting you!

4.  Can you relate?
Reflect & Support
Hello ,

There are a lot of really great posts in our facebook group. If you already wrote a post, or left a comment - thanks!  If you have not introduced yourself yet, what are you waiting for?

Between the facebook posts & the email messages people have sent me, I've noticed a few common themes.
 
What challenges do people in our group face?
  • Some are looking for ways to keep up with the demands of a career and family life.
  • Some are still on parental leave and are anxious about going back to work.
  • Some want to find new employment in Vienna, but don’t know where to start.
  • Some are thinking about starting their own business.
  • Some are struggling with the MA35.
  • Some are seeking opportunities to network and build social contacts with like-minded people.

With this in mind, I want to look at the second pillar of our three core values - Reflect.

Our second value, Reflect, is essential to VFN Professionals and gives purpose to our activities. Without reflection, the other pieces can easily become busy work and just another thing to add to an already too long list of tasks for the week. Who needs that??

As a member of VFN Professionals, you owe it to yourself to reflect on your life and your reasons for being here. What is your situation and what challenges do you face? If you have not already considered this question, take a few moments to do it now. 
 
When you have a moment, look over some of the posts from others. Can you relate? Is there something that you know about that might add value here? 

Hit the like button. Leave a comment. Let us know your thoughts.




5. quarter goals
Creating Real Value
Hello !
Today I want to continue talking about the second of our three core values - Reflect - and encourage you to think again about why you joined our group.

How does this group add value to your life?

As a member of VFN Professionals, you owe it to yourself to reflect on your life and your reasons for being here. This group is here for your benefit and by building up your own skills you will help raise the level of the group as a whole. Think about what you want to accomplish and what you want from the group. Some of you have already written to me saying:
I want to 
  • refresh my CV
  • build up my confidence 
  • negotiate better work conditions
  • find a more complete child care solution
  • boost my professional networking skills
  • get time management solutions that really work
  • connect with like-minded people
  • save my social life
Take a few moments now to reflect on your current situation and answer this question:

"In the next quarter, what are the 2-3 biggest issues that I want to have solved?"

When answering this question, focus on what you want to accomplish in the next 3 months. What are the most important and immediate solutions that you need?  These should be short term or pressing goals. We'll call this list your Quarter Targets.

Share your Quarter Targets in our facebook group or send it to me by email. Think of this as your first step towards finding a solution.

I'll go first - here are my Quarter Targets:
1. I want to create a child-safe play area in my home. (So I can focus on other things besides damage control.)
2. Identify the needs and pains of my target audience. (For product development)

IMPORTANT: Don't wait until later to create your Quarter Targets! We both know that this email will simply get lost somewhere down at the bottom of your inbox. If you are honest with yourself, it should only take 2 minutes to know what the biggest topics are for you at the moment.  So make a quick list, it doesn't have to be perfect, you can always update it or even change it later.

These lists are useful for building smaller networks within our group and will help guide our plans for upcoming meetings and workshops.

I can't wait to see your Quarter Targets!

Today I would like to continue talking about our third value - PERFECT.

Once you have determined how you wish to make changes and what outcomes you want, the next big hurdle is making it actually happen, right?

Tell me if you can relate to this: When trying to make changes, sometimes I feel like I am caught in a hamster wheel. At times I make a lot of noise and try a lot of different things, but nothing seems to work out right. Other times I am distracted, or worse completely paralysed by indecision about which steps to actually take. In the end, the result seems to be the same; time goes by and I feel no closer to my goals than when I started. So frustrating!

How can I get off the hamster wheel?
One of the best solutions I have found to break out of the hamster wheel is reaching out to other people. This can mean reaching out to a friend or loved one. Usually, however, I find it is better to reach out to someone new, a person with new ideas and perspectives. Someone who can help shake things up a bit and help me get unstuck. Sometimes this means finding a mentor, an accountability partner or both! Networking and reaching out to others has helped me create valuable relationships and build up the support system I need while living abroad. 

VFN Professionals is an extension of this idea. As a group we represent many different countries, cultures and industries, and we all chose to live and work here in Vienna with our families.    

Today I encourage you to think about your support network. In general, you are the average of the five people that you surround yourself with. Are you getting the support and encouragement that you want? Or are you stuck in a hamster wheel?
