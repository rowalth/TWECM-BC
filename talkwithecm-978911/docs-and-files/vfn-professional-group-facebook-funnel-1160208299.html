VFN professional group - facebook & funnel

FACEBOOK POST:

Hello everyone!
I want to start a new VFN group - a VFN Professionals group. This group will focus on questions around the topic "How can I be seen as a professional and have a family at the same time?" 

We will discuss diverse topics such as:
- How to answer questions about holes (i.e., parental leave) in your CV.
- How to look and feel professional with baby food stains on your shirt.
- Where to find reliable childcare when kindergarten is not available?
- and more!

For a first step, I would like to create a subgroup here on facebook where we can join together to ask questions, exchange ideas and grow. Later, I imagine live meet ups or workshops based on topics that are important to us as a group. 

As this group is meant to be for VFN members and by VFN members, I would like to hear your ideas. What questions do you have? What would help you succeed? 

Are you interested in supporting a VFN Professionals group?

Connect

Today, I want to talk to you about the first value: Connect.

This group makes it easy for you to connect with others who understand the challenges you face as both a professional and an international family living in Vienna. 

Often when people face struggles, they feel alone, confused and don't even know where to find support. I've seen even highly skilled professional people feel stuck and frustrated, desperately looking for solutions. I created this group so that people can find information when they want to save their career, bring harmony to their family, resurrect their social life or (yikes!) all three. 

At VFN Professionals we understand what you are going through because many of us have faced the same challenges. By discussing and walking through topics with us, you too can get unstuck and find real solutions. As a group we can share resources and support from experience and insider knowledge. You are not alone, you have found your tribe.

In the spirit of connecting, take a moment to think about others you know who could benefit from connecting with our group as well. Today I want to continue talking about the second of our three core values - Reflect - and encourage you to think about why you joined our group.

How does this group add value to your life?

As a member of VFN Professionals, you owe it to yourself to reflect on your life and your reasons for being here. This group is here for your benefit and by building up your own skills you will help raise the level of the group as a whole. Think about what you want to accomplish and what you want from the group.

Do you want to:
- refresh you CV
- find a mentor
- negotiate better work conditions
- build up your online presence
- boost your professional networking skills
- get time management solutions that really work
- make a big change in your professional life
- connect with like-minded people

Take a few moments to reflect on your current situation and answer this question:

In the next quarter, what are the 2-3 biggest topics that I want to have solved?

When answering this question, focus on what you want to accomplish in the next 3 months. What are the most important and immediate solutions that you need?  These should be short term or pressing goals. We'll call this list your Quarter List.

Often speaking out our goals and sharing them with others is the first step towards finding a solution.

Share your Quarter List in our facebook group or send it to me by email. These lists are useful for building smaller networks within our group and will help guide our plans for upcoming meetings and workshops.

IMPORTANT: Don't wait until later to create your Quarter List and don't let this email get lost somewhere down at the bottom of your inbox. If you are honest with yourself, it should only take 2 minutes to know what the biggest topics are for you at the moment.  So make a quick list, you can always update it later.

I can't wait to see your Quarter List!
