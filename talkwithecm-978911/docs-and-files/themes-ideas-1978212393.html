  • Key content 
1. Two Key Mind shifts - where your focus should be when approaching / talking to someone
  • adding value
    • is my shirt wrinkled, is wrinkled cool? ugg - no!
    • What value can I bring to this situation? What can I add?
  • feelings
    • Maya Angelou - quote
    • focus on how you want the other person to feel
    • this may be an extra challenge for introverts - we get so caught up in our own heads...but tonight - focus on the other person and how they feel.
2. What to do?
  • First impressions start with the first LOOK
+open body language
hands out (not in pockets / crossed) shoulders down & back
eye contact / smile

+Go to the watering hole - this is the place where animals regularly drink - a meeting space. As humans, we are not so different:
  • at work - people tend to meet around the water cooler, coffee machine,
  • at home - kitchen
  • party or networking event - by the food / bar!, or in this case the Pop Up travel shop :)
+Be first - 60 second challenge

3. words - what am I going to say??
    • Pick up lines - "Your feet must be tired, because you've been running through my mind ALL night!" uhh...no.
    • Hi, I'm Elizabeth. - really, research shows that this is the most successful opening line, especially if your name is Elizabeth!
    • What's your name? / How are you? / Come here often?
    • "be kind whenever possible. It is always possible" Dali Lama
    • being kind means acknowledge / respect other people,
