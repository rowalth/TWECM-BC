The Plot Twist Email (new bonus/module/payment plan): Sales Email #14-17

Note from Raelyn: This is a more subtle sales email. If you prefer to send a sales email that is more “indirect”, this plot twist email is what you need. The Plot Twist Email starts off with an excuse to email your list because something has changed - i.e. the “plot twist”. In this email, you don’t directly sell, but you indirectly mention your product / service and what’s happening with it. I call it the plot twist email because something has to change (aka a plot twist) that warrants an email to your list.
Here are some examples of plot twists you can use to email your list about... and bring your launch / promotional campaign back to the top of their minds!

●  I changed something in the course
●  I had to redo everything in the course
●  I had to redo certain chapters of the course
●  New bonus added
●  New training added
●  Training removed
●  Payment plan added

Subject Line: [Describe the Plot Twist Here - e.g. I Redid 2 Modules of [Course Name]...] Email Copy:
Hey [first name]!
How have you been?

I have been working tirelessly for the last few days because I... [what’s the plot twist?]
(E.g. Woah, I have been working tirelessly for the last few days because I had to unexpectedly re-do 2 modules of [Course Name].)

This is crazy because as you know, we are in the middle of a [sale / launch / etc - why you are busy now] but I had to drop everything I was doing in order to [describe plot twist again]. Here’s the crazy story behind why I decided to... [explain why the plot twist had to happen (in this case, why you decided to change things up]
I am confident that [plot twist] will help so many more of you to [end goal]!

For those of you who haven’t heard about [Course Name] (ahh!), it is my [what the course is about] that will help you to [end goal].

Click here to learn more about [Course Name]. [link to sales page]

P.S. In [#] days, the cart will close for [Course Name!] and the price will increase to $[#]... so hop on now!

Sales Email #16: New Payment Plan Added (Plot Twist Email 2)

Note from Raelyn: This is an email that you can send to your list to prompt a spike in sales for any promotion you are doing - whether it is a launch or evergreen campaign. This is especially useful if you have a pricier product or service. There will always be a group of people who couldn’t afford your product but wanted to purchase it. Others may be hesitant to pay a huge sum of money up-front (even if they have the cash) and this payment plan will increase the number of students who choose to take you up on your offer. You can also choose to send this email after the cart has closed, giving people who have missed your promotion another chance to buy. Personally, I don’t send this email, but students who have done so have reported amazing results.

Subject Line: New Payment Plan Added!

Email Copy:

Did you want to enroll in [Course Name] but can’t afford to right now?

I have heard you, [first name]!

We’ve added a new payment plan for [Course Name]. If you wanted to join [Course Name] but didn’t have a chance to do so, now you can! [link to sales page]

With the new payment plan, you can get started with just $[#], followed by [#] payments of $[#]. The cart is open for another 24 hours to give those of you who want to get on the new payment plan a chance to hop on!
I don’t want anybody to miss the chance to [end goal].
If this is what you need right now, click here to enroll now with the new payment plan! [link to sales page]
To your success,
