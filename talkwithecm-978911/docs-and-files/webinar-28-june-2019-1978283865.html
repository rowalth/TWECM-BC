Free Webinar - talkwithecm.com - Elizabeth Miller

Are you ready to learn how to make a great first impression and start building business relationships that matter? This webinar gives you the building blocks to talk to anyone, anywhere, at any time… whether it’s one-on-one or in a group setting, whether you're trying to get a job or build a friendship.

On Friday, June 28, I'm hosting a free LIVE webinar on how to talk to anyone.
Register right here on this page.


What if you could talk to anyone, anytime, anywhere using the power of body language to create an instant connection?

Here's an email from one member "I was so tired of waiting. So I took a job that was below my skill level. I thought it would be fine to start low just to get my foot in the door and take on more responsibilities later. I really wanted do something with my career...but I didn't know how to start. When I tried talking to people about it I felt awkward. I said the wrong things at the wrong time and people would just stare. It was awful! I felt so stuck."   

Sound familiar? And it is not only about talking to people you already know. How many of us have felt this way when talking to people we don't know - how about networking events, or interviews? Or what about making a presentation or big pitch for work?

Although the situations change, the story is the same: Unlike those people who always seem to know exactly the right things to say, when we say something, it comes out completely wrong. Or worse - we don't say anything at all.

If there is anything worse than saying the wrong things, it is saying nothing at all and being forgettable and staying stuck. No one wants to be forgettable, and feeling awkward isn't that great either.

Listen to this women's comment: "As a global citizen, I've moved around a lot, and now that I have children, well, there is a big gap in my CV. I used to get so irritated when people asked about the gap in my CV, but now after working with Elizabeth, it's my favorite question! I learned how to answer in a way that feels natural for me and highlights my skills." 

Whether getting ready for an interview, giving a presentation at work, or looking to connect with new people - it feels so good when you know exactly what to do. I'm talking about all those nitty-gritty details like where to stand, how to make good eye contact, who talks first, what to say and all those things that can get in the way of what you want. I'd like show you how to do all of this and more.

That's why I'm so excited to offer you my How to talk to anyone webinar.
This webinar is for people who want to improve the way that they talk and achieve the success and confidence that comes along with that.



What you get in the webinar:
  • the exact words, body language, and even subtle “connection” strategies to use.
  • a roll out strategy - step by step guide for changing the way you interact and developing your own style, 3 challenges
  • confidence boosters
  • the 3 things you need to know BEFORE you enter the room
  • a simple framework so that you'll never be at a loss for words again
  • word for word scripts to start / end / engage 


topics:
  • Focus on Value - Focus on feeling confident about the value you are adding in any given situation. Everyone can do it (restaurant tip/knowing where the bathroom is/etc.) this is where you can hang your confidence on.

  • Be PROACTIVE
    • Take a few minutes to prepare yourself BEFORE you enter the room
      • Ask yourself - What value can I offer in this situation?
      • Develop a strong 10 second introduction
      • Prepare yourself with a few topics
        • Something in the news / tending on social media
        • Something specific to this group of people
        • Avoid controversial topics
      • Set your intention and stay focused on reaching your goal
      • The art of small talk 
        • Use the secret messages behind outward body cues to start conversations
        • Sample scripts with the exact words you can use to start a conversation
        • Take an active role to help the conversation flow in the direction you want
        • Keep the conversation exciting 
        • Use a clean forward thinking exit
        • Follow up as promised
      • Frameworks for approaching people
        • Ask yourself: How do I want this person/people to feel when talking to me? entertained/valued/attracted - maya angelo quote
        • 60 second challenge - challenge yourself to introduce yourself to someone (anyone) within 60 seconds of walking into a room.
        • Positive Feedback challenge - challenge yourself to give someone a genuine compliment (something they are wearing, or something they did).
  • Control what people think when they first meet you
    • Be aware of engagement cues - Is the person really listening or simply waiting for another chance to speak?
      • manage engagement over time
      • Look for cues to deepen engagement & build connection
      • TOOL: chart (engagement over time)
    • Branching off opportunities that help you expertly shift the conversation flow
    • Use pauses & silence effectively to sound more professional
    • Use sample scripts to start, shape & end conversations
    • How to be more likeable & more popular
      • Effectively use body language & personal space to deliver your deeper message
        • move only with intention & purpose
      • Be aware of micro-expressions use them to your advantage -  Watch for OH moments!
      • Utilize proven eye contact techniques to help build trust and create intimacy 
      • Leverage non-verbal cues 
        • Smiling - This is a great way to connect with others, but over doing it can seem fake or even desperate.
        • Nodding - This is a great way to show engagement, but over doing has the same risk as smiling too much. 
        • Eye Contact - Eyes are like a window into the soul of a person. What are your listeners eyes telling you?
