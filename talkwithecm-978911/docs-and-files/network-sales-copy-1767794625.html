Network sales copy



Hey ecm,





Do you ever feel you need a LOT more time to get comfortable or think about what to say in a conversation? More than most people? And you don’t understand how other people seem to do it so easily.



I *definitely* do! 


Because I want time to think, I'm not so good at networking, making small talk, or meeting new people. Sometimes I feel too tired. I don't know what to say. And I don't enjoy conversations about sports or the weather. 


The truth is, I'm naturally shy and I'm introverted. 




You too? If you have the same feelings — plus, you feel that networking *in English* is particularly challenging — then I have some good news.


It doesn't have to be so hard. Here's how I know:



I've learned how to network in a way that's comfortable for me. And I want to help you do the same so you can meet new people, make friends, and build relationships in English more easily.






In this lesson, you'll learn how to:


  • Use your strengths as an introvert for effective networking in English
  • Make small mindset changes that will help you enjoy chatting with others in English
  • Prepare English questions and phrases you can use to get your started 

At the end, I've got a challenge for you. It's the perfect opportunity to practice what you've learned. You can share your answers with me, get feedback, and learn from others in the Confident English Community by sharing in the comment section at the end of the lesson.



Plus, I'd love to hear your stories about networking in English. 


I look forward to hearing from you, 


Annemarie
