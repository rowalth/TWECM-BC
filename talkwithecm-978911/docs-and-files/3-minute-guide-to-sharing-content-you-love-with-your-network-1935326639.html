3 minute guide to sharing content you love with your network


How to share content you love with your network


  1. Find content that adds value for your audience

  2. Structure the content and inject your personality into the content. 


  3. Create a compelling title. 


  4. Write an introduction to invite readers in and and a concluding statement to really drive home the main message.


  5. Include insider information or current events. 


  6. Ensure the article is optimized for readability. 


  7. Optimize the keywords for search engines. 


  8. Add a clear call-to-action and let them know your next desired step - even if "no response is needed"
