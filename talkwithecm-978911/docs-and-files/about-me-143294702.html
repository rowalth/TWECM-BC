About Me

Hello, 
My name is Elizabeth. My full name is Elizabeth Catherine Miller - I am the ecm of talk with ecm! 

I empower professionals to overcome hurdles and dramatically improve their English language skills and accomplish their career goals. Today, I am ready to help you meet your goals - this could mean: 
  • pass an important exam 
  • increase your salary 
  • open new business markets 
  • get a new job 
  • open new business opportunities 
  • & more! 

I take a different approach than most other English teachers... 

I don’t use textbooks filled with a lot of grammar rules and drills. Instead, I show you how to focus on your goals, develop the key points of learning and master the skills you need to move forward. 

I don’t have all the answers, in fact many times, I have all the questions! I show you how to think about the questions & find the answers you need. I encourage English learners to share their knowledge & skills together.  

I focus on speaking - together - right away. I will show you the tools & tips you need to speak confidently in English from day 1. With this knowledge you can train your brain to work like a native English speaker.
