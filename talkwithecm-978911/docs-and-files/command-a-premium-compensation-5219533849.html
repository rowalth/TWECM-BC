Command a Premium Compensation

There are 4 main reasons why someone is willing to compromise and accept lower compensation:
  1. They really want to get out of their current job
  2. They are struggling, feel desperate & they think it is going to be easier if they drop their salary expectations. 
  3. They question if their expertise & experience is worth it
  4. Or, they think it is worth it, but they don't know how to negotiate a higher salary and they are afraid that they won't get it.

The real reason why you need to demand premium compensation:
The compensation you get is directly related to the value the company puts on this position. Compensation - strategic input = IMPACT
Consider not only the value that you offer, but add on the value that you can CREATE = compensation

Have a strategy - not a lost duck
Most people don't have a strategy:

  • People would be looking for a new challenge, but, end up in exactly the same job just in another company. 
  • So many people just drift from job to job and never having a career.
  •  So many people accept a job only to realise it’s the same B.S as in their previous company. 
  • And most people STOP believing they CAN have a fulfilling job, and they end up settling for a job that underutilises, undervalues and underpays them. 
  • All because they didn’t have the right strategy or plan to get the job and career they really wanted... 

Without a strategy you end up accepting a job because you didn’t have any other option.... (and you desperately wanted to get out of your other job)


Strategy:
level 1. Starts with YOU: Mindset shift, UVP - Unique Value Proposition (Pitch, values, purpose), mastering self-promotion without playing a role
level 2. Your Communications: Marketing Material - CV/resume, Online Profiles, Written outreach - cover letter
level 3. Out in the field: job searching, networking, interviewing (informational & job interviews), raising your profile, negotiation
