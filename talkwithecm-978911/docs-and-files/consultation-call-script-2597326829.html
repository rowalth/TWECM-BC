consultation call script

15-30 minutes
Free

I'm going to ask questions to determine if/how I can help you. 

  • what is it that YOU want to accomplish
  • what is holding you back
  • recommended solutions
Agree that at the end of the call - give a yes/no if the solution works

My main GOAL:
  1. Where are you on journey?
    1. So, tell me more about....what's not working?
    2. why not?
    3. how is this impacting XYZ?
    4. how does that make you feel?
    5. how much longer are you willing to struggle with that?
    6. are you ready to change that today?
  2. what do you want to accomplish in the future
    1. Let's switch gears, what exactly do you want your (work/life) to look like 3-6 months from now? (a few goals here!)
    2. how would it feel to accomplish this goal?
    3. what would it mean to accomplish this goal?
    4. what else would it mean to you?
    5. What else would you like to accomplish/change/have/be different?
  3. what is holding you back?
    1. Excellent, now the thing is, obviously you aren't going to get that (work/life) you want if you continue doing things the way that you have been doing them, right?
    2. So, what is holding you back from accomplishing (XYZ)
    3. How is that holding you back? How is that having an impact on your (life/work)?
    4. How does it make you feel, knowing that XYZ is having an impact on your (work/life/health) and (what exactly the impact is) & how that is holding you back from (accomplishing goal)?
    5. How much longer are you willing to continue struggling with that?
    6. Are you ready to change that today?
    7. What else is holding you back? (or suggest a few typical stuff)
  4. What is the impact of NOT accomplishing your goal? (see above)
  5. What will it mean for you to accomplish this goal? (see above)
Recommendations:
  • She needs something I can't provide - be honest. 
  • She only needs some advice I can give in the call - give it!
  • She needs some info product or training I have.
  • She need some 1:1 coaching - specialized
  • She needs coaching program.
I can help you overcome (problems) & achieve (goal). Would you like me to tell you about my (solution name)?

If I can show you that by (solution) you will be able to solve (challenges) and achieve (desired results!) and it is affordable to you, are you ready to get started today?
  • Not today - How long are you willing to continue doing things the same way, before making a change? What is holding you back from getting started today? 
    • Alternative step - Suggest ideas, ways to make changes/get ready & come back on the phone when ready. 
    • STOP
  • YES - describe program!
