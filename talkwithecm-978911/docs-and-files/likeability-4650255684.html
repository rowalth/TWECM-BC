Likeability

1. compliment on a task, skill, something EARNED, or even something they are struggling with
  • vs. smile / eyes...not something earned
  • notice something new / extra effort
  • recognize / value what they are doing
  • example - valentines day alles gurgle
2. Get them to talk about what they love
  • How did you like it?
  • oh really? what did you love about it?
  • active listening, get the positive feelings flowing
3. Share a truth that is to your detriment
  • HONESTY FIRST 
  • Miracle on 34th street - St. Nick, sends customers elsewhere...
  • Stepfather Dave, uses this same principle & has some of the most loyal customers in the world...one family even chose to move to the town where his shop is, just because of their experience with him
4. Open body language
  • hands in pockets/crossed arms
  • show open hands, open arms
5. exceed expectations without asking for permission - the surprise element, picking up coffee, dinner...sure you could ask, in fact asking to do a favor is polite, kind, but doing a favor without asking is mind blowing!

6. Be the first person to joke
  • cuts the tension early on, establish the human connection
  • most of us appreciate humor, laughter & we're starving for it!
