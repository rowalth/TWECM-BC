3 day challenge notes

Day 1 Challenge:

Build bigger mice, not a better mousetrap

They need to understand what happens if they DON’T solve the problem: magnify the fear, pinpoint

Solve a very clear, BURNING point - need to spend more time increasing the pain

Show them what a mice-less world would look like - tell them they DESERVE this existence and only we can get them there

The offer is what makes people say yes : they are not buying the product, they are buying peace of mind

7 Offer Angles
(Segment the audience for each of these)
1. **Rescue Offer (audience is in a really shitty place: I am going to give you a plan and the tools and pull you out)
2. **The undiscovered gold offer (possibility of something even better and guide them towards a treasure map/eventual dream - I have a way, I have a system - you don’t know it, but it’s there, I’ll take you there)
3. Co-Creation Offer (let us hold you by the hand as you do it rather than let me show you step by step)
4. Hungry Caterpillar - create hunger for the rest by a piece for free (if the free content is this good, how good is the paid? - what they’ve seen means they cannot cancel their membership! )
5. Let me prove it (do it live and watch people see that it works - no competition here: we are going to do it: case study)
6. Yay without the boo - create a minor annoyance and the offer
7. Market disruption offer - unheard of in your industry

1. Lock the right delivery mechanism
2. Enhance perceived value of offer
3. use the right PITCH
4. use the right Buyer Qualifier

Include this:
*the outcome they crave
*the outcome they want to avoid
*what is their before state
*what is their after state
*what is the minimum time frame
*what are the mental blocks that stops them from investing
*what is the benefit apart from competition

Tell them who it is NOT for

Day 2 Challenge:

Do I believe my offers are life-changing? How can we make this even better and something we want to join and feel excited about and is truly life-changing?

It is my responsibility to give someone this amazing, life-changing, awesome content.

Rules of endless eyeballs:
1. Be obsessed with you - your offer, solution, sorry, your signature, your understanding (Be OBSESSED and contagious with YOUR offer - fireworks coming out and you are super excited)
2. Seek to understand: people don’t buy from people who THEY understand; people buy from people who make them FEEL understood : “You Get Me”; don’t be like understand my offer, me, the sale - you need to understand THEM; how do you get people to look at you and be like “You Get Me”
3. Be shameless - be relentless, shameless, and proud (you’re not ready to sell a crap ton if you are not shameless)

Think like the fish, not the fisherman - it won’t sell because YOU relate to it.

Places to Find EyeballsL

1. Your own social media (IG, LinkedIn, FB)
2. Other’s social media (Fb groups, IG, etc., Clubhouse)
3. Guest blogging (Try this! - pitch it to places and publish; establish authority)
4. Giveaways/contests
5. Podcasts
6. Joint ventures (collaborating with others at the same level)
7. Sponsored posts/shout outs (Instead of spending ads only - IG posts; ex: womenceo IG, etc.)
8. Affiliate traffic (commission/cross-promotion)
9. Customized content w/sale split (serving same audience but offer is different: create it just for the group + exclusive; and other way: if your audience needs something that you don’t sell: have someone else bring it in)

Hook the Eyeballs:

Attention is the currency of the internet

1. Unique signature system (have a branded framework: our 4 step training)
2. Borrowed authority (featured in this/worked in this)
3. Social proof (testimonials/quotes in ads + customers’ video ads)
4. Own authority (people you’ve helped/money you’ve made; podcast)
5. A big secret revealed
6. Diagnostic assessment (identify the problem for someone - a quiz or something; people believe something is wrong and want to know what it is)
7. Hand on pulse (metrics, industry, etc. - be the first one talking about news)
8. Disruption
