more stuff

1. charisma & likability (trustworthiness, intelligence, leadership)
people decide / first impression happen in the first 7 seconds - yikes! that's fast
No words need to be said.

what matters?
  • hand gestures (intention / explain)
  • vocal energy -how exciting is it?
  • facial engagement - if you're happy & your know it - smile
Want people to talk more?
  • face them (full body)
  • Ask HOW/WHY questions
  • slight head tilt
  • triple nod
Be influential - it is not an on / off switch
  • people remember how you made them feel (& everyone wants to feel valued!) 
  • add value to the conversation 
    • (if I just told a story, what about you?)

Be the best you can be:
  • embrace your imperfections, If you feel nervous - it can be a bonding point
  • don't be a conversational narcissist
  • How you can ask the most interesting questions
    • what question can you ask, to get the other person to tell their story
    • not 'what do you do?' Who is the most interesting person you've ever met?
  • Gush, don't gossip, spontaneous trait transference...
    • she's so mean, so awful - sticks to me! associated with me
    • genuine gushing / bragging 
    • all of the adjectives that you = STICK to you!
  • Show your hands! Keep them above the table. :)
    • keep them in the box
  • Deep gazing
    • Notice their eye color
