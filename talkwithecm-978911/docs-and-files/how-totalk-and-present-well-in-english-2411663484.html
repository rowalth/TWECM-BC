How toTalk and present well in English

Three steps one become comfortable speaking English,

1 that means practice and get it flowing out of your mouth get used to using words that you don’t usually use make your active for capillary more robust

2. Repair and structure, it’s better to have fewer ideas and develop them more. Listeners don’t want to hear a list of ideas they want to hear how you analyze and process and put those ideas together give context and meaning and support for your statements in general it’s better to have 2 to 4 ideas so as not to overwhelm your listeners. You’ll notice I have three ideas in this article

3. Now that you’ve prepared your speaking abilities your vocabulary abilities and the content of what you want to talk about and structured what you want to say now it’s about presenting it well and presenting yourself with confidence using the right tone of voice meaningful hand gestures and body language
