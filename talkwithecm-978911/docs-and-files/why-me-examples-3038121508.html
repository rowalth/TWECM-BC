Why Me?!
---------
Don’t get fooled by this picture. It took me years to get comfortable with being front and center with my business.
I went through years of telling myself,
That only my work will speak for itself, I don’t need to.
  That people will get what I do because it is sooo obvious.
  That I will be seen as a bragger if I put out the results I’m getting.
That I will come across as too salesy if I put out my offers.
That visibility will get me trolls and people I don’t want to deal with.
It was safe for me to hide behind my business name. It was safe to hide my real self. It was safe to be in the wings and have no one ever know my genius.
Ironic, since I am in the business of building brands. And never built my own.
I have been a Branding and Communication Specialist for 17 years and worked with 200+ very large and small businesses.
So a year ago, I decided to put all my knowledge to the test and started building out my own network and brand.
I quickly found out Branding your own self is a lot harder than most people think. Because it’s difficult to see your own genius and NOT underrate it.
So I put together a system for myself that made it super simple. I started using it and seeing people reach out to me. Slowly I started giving it to my clients.
And the results have been amazing....
I have had people tell me they’d rather save money to work with me 1:1 than work with someone cheaper.
One client chased me for 3 months, before I said yes to work with him.
Some of my clients said they filled out their programs like “magic”
Another client had people reach out to them for collaborations
No, it’s not all the 100 things the marketing people tell you you need, and it’s definitely not that pretty logo, colors etc (I say this even though I am a trained designer).
And I’m not going to tell you to do video everyday even if you have nothing great to say.
It is actually an intentional, deliberate, carefully crafted strategy to build a particular perception and a reputation. That’s what building a BRAND really means.
Every piece of communication is a part of a larger strategy and has a deliberate outcome. If done right, it builds you a line of prospects and gets you to close sales faster.
Getting visible is one very important component of Branding. But it is NOT the only thing.
If you want to learn how to build your BRAND the right way, without burning yourself out creating random content or spending what’s left of your life on social media, reach out to me and let’s talk.
