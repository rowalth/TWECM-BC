Day 1: How to Build a Bulletproof Mindset in 2021

  1. Closed vs. growth mindset
  2. Skills vs. IQ (hard work, connections, relationship building, sales, introvert, socially awkward, etc. )
  3. 365 days - you only need about 30 good days to be successful !!
  4. Imposter syndrome? Wanting to look the part? Forget it. BIG NAMES aren’t showing off their stuff…straight up honesty & quality - not fancy cars. Deliver quality, stand on your foundation of VALUE & then put forward your EFFORT.
  5. THE STRUGGLE - don’t quit, don’t shy away from the responsibility, don’t shy away from it - EMBRACE THIS STRUGGLE MAKES YOU SUCCESSFUL
  6. haters…negativity…especially digitally…it PART OF THIS GAME - they are there BECAUSE you are on the right path
  7. You think that you have a good mindset…is it reflected in your success? A growth mindset will keep pushing you, growing, reaching…Life will always test this, are you defined by your mistakes? Your shortcomings? Or by your experiences adapting, pivoting, reinvesting? 
  8. What’s the fire that is driving you? Do you love winning? Or hate losing ? What drives you?
  9. Get help - a good coach…it’s like a dr. You’re sick…I’m not going to a dr…it’s too expensive. That’s ridiculous, find someone who knows what they are doing & can tell you what you need to hear, not what you want to hear, but what you need to hear. “I’ll get a coach when I get more successful = that’s backwards!” Be COACHABLE! Don’t be fixed in your mind (waste of time/ money), this is not about raisin picking…!
  10. George Ross Celebrity Apprentice Judge - coach tip to D. Trump upon getting the nomination - Learn how to admit that you’ve made a mistake. <- HUGE - people like you, it is human, real. Big life lesson - learn how to talk to & negotiate with EVERYONE! 
  11. What is success? Define it - money? Security? Relationships (business/personal)? Giving back to community? Most just look at the money - the number of 0’s in your bank account…it’s much at the end of the day.  As for me - I want to add value to the world, and more importantly, to specific people, individuals that I helped and make their life better. 
  12. Body language - listen to what people are NOT saying - comfort level, fears, etc. 
  13. What is happiness? Study Harvard 50+ years - mentor give info to next generation / those that stick to the difficult one in the family / sustained relationship
