Talks 


"How I built my first website with WordPress and turned the site into a thriving online business"  by Philipp Alexander Stelzel 


Philipp Alexander will tell us how he built his first website with WordPress and turned marathon-vorbereitung.com into a thriving online business.

This talks will be held in German or English – depending on the audience. 

"A recap of WordCamp Europe in Paris"  by Radoslav Georgiev & Thomas Kräftner

WordCamp Europe is an annual conference and also the biggest WordPress event in the world. It takes place in a different city each year. After Vienna last year WCEU took place in Paris this year. 

Radoslav and Thomas were there and will share their experience of this years WordCamp. They'll also highlight some of the most interesting talks they've seen and what they've learned.
